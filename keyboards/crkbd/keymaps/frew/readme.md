## fREW's Corne Keyboard Layout

### Layer 0

 combo | tap        | hold
 ----- | ---------- | ----
 `FN1` | `a`        | Layer 1
 `FN2` | `z`        | Layer 2
 `FN3` | Escape     | Control
 `FN4` | `/`        | Layer 2
 `FN5` | `;`        | Layer 1
 `FN6` | Play/Pause | Left Shift
 `FN7` | Enter      | MOD
 `LED` | Toggles Backlight Settings |
```

,-----------------------------------.              ,-----------------------------------.
|  `  |  q  |  w  |  e  |  r  |  t  |              |  y  |  u  |  i  |  o  |  p  |  -  |
|-----+-----+-----+-----+-----+-----|              |-----+-----+-----+-----+-----+-----|
| TAB | FN1 |  s  |  d  |  f  |  g  |              |  h  |  j  |  k  |  l  | FN5 |  '  |
|-----+-----+-----+-----+-----+-----+              |-----+-----+-----+-----+-----+-----|
| FN6 | FN2 |  x  |  c  |  v  |  b  |              |  n  |  m  |  ,  |  .  | FN4 |RSFT |
`-----+-----+-----+-----+-----+-----+-----.  ,-----+-----+-----|-----+-----+-----+-----'
                        | BSPC| MOD | FN3 |  |LED  | FN7 | SPC |
                        `-----------------'  `-----+-----+-----'
```
